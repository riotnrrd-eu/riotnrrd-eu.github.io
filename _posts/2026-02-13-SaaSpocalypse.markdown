Is GenAI the death of enterprise SaaS? Or are we just seeing its latest evolution?  
  
Big transitions like this one are forcing functions for change, and GenAI will definitely bring a lot of that — but to think that people are just going to vibe-code every tool they need at the moment they need it is to misunderstand what enterprise software is and why it is valuable in the first place.
